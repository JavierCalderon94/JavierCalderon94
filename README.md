# Hi, I'm Javier Calderón 👋

## About Me

I'm a business and engineering professional who found his way into data through a genuine curiosity about how numbers can drive better decisions. With a background in **Business Administration** and **Industrial Organization Engineering**, I spent several years in sales operations and customer service — building reports, managing data pipelines in ERP systems, and connecting teams across Sales, Accounting and Logistics.

That experience made one thing clear: the real value wasn't in the operations themselves, but in what the data behind them could tell you. So I completed a **Data Science & AI Bootcamp at The Bridge** and haven't looked back since.

I'm currently pursuing an **MBA in Pharmaceutical Industry & Healthcare at CESIF**, combining my analytical skills with a deep dive into Market Access, pricing strategies and digital health — with the goal of applying data to meaningful problems in the healthcare sector.
